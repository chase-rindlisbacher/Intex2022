Our Approach:

Our goal is to help educate, create a community of support, and provide rewards to kidney disease patients as they make lifestyle changes. We also strive to help existing patients continue to live a healthy lifestyle with those same goals!

For the best experience, please create an account or login!

Learn More

Educate

Not a food or exercise guru? We've got your back!

Login to your account or provide us with a few anonymous details and we can customize your kidney diet to help you reach your goals.

Support

Studies show that friendship makes lifestyle changes easier.

We provide you a way to be part of a customizable community that helps each other meet lifestyle goals.

Reward

You should be rewarded for all your hard work!

The more goals you achieve, the more points you receive that can be converted into the prizes of your choice!
